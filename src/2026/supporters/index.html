Supporters

We’re a non-profit organisation, and we’re committed to keep Inclusive Design 24 free for all participants. This is only possible thanks to our generous supporters – with their help, we're able to make sure all the talks are captioned, and to offer all our presenters and guest hosts a modest "thank you" / honorarium for their time and effort. Without our supporters, the spirit of #id24 would be hard to keep alive.

Inclusive Design 24 offers four supporter tiers: bronze, silver, gold, and platinum.

Perk Bronze Silver Gold Platinum
Logo and link on the #id24 site Bronze Silver Gold Platinum
Mentions on our social networks before, during and after #id24 Bronze Silver Gold Platinum
Mention at the start and end of every talk Gold Platinum
Your own custom sponsor message on our social networks Gold Platinum

Join us live for the pre-show / welcome session Platinum
