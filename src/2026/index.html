Save the date!
#id24 will return on 24 September 2026

Inclusive Design 24 (#id24) is a free 24-hour online event for the global community. It celebrates inclusive design and shares knowledge and ideas from analogue to digital, from design to development, from planners to practitioners, and everything and everyone in between.

Call for presentations

Have a great idea for a talk? Want to present at #id24? Great! Our call for presentations is now open.

Submit a talk

The call for presentations closes on 31 May 2026. Selected speakers will be notified the week of 22 June 2026.

Have a great talk in mind, but English is not your first language? No problem – we're happy to accept submissions for talks in other languages. For sessions that are not in English, we will include secondary translated captions in English after the event.

Have any questions? Just drop us an email at hello@inclusivedesign24.org

How to join

No sign-up. No registration. All sessions are streamed live and publicly on the Inclusive Design 24 YouTube channel{% if 2026.playlistId != blank %} – see the entire playlist for the event{% endif %}.

All sessions include automated live closed captions. After the event, these are replaced by manually edited closed captions. Unfortunately, we are not able to provide sign language interpreters for the event.
