Supporters

We're a non-profit organisation, and we're able to keep Inclusive Design 24 free for all participants, to make sure all the talks are captioned in real-time, and to offer all our presenters and guest hosts a "thank you" for their time and effort, because of the generosity of our supporters. Without their help, the spirit of #id24 would be hard to keep alive.

Inclusive Design 24 offers four supporter tiers: bronze, silver, gold, and platinum.

Perk Bronze Silver Gold Platinum
Logo and link on the #id24 site Bronze Silver Gold Platinum
Mentions on social networks before, during and after #id24 Bronze Silver Gold Platinum
Mention at the start and end of every talk Gold Platinum
A sponsor message on @id24conf Gold Platinum
Logo on all session thumbnails and bumpers Platinum
Join us live for the pre-show / welcome session Platinum

Interested in becoming a supporter? Just drop us an email at hello@inclusivedesign24.org
