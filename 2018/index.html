Save the date... #id24 returns on 11 October 2018!

Inclusive Design 24 (#id24) is a free 24-hour online event for the global community. It celebrates inclusive design and shares knowledge and ideas from analogue to digital, from design to development, from planners to practitioners, and everything and everyone in between.

Tweets by id24conf

How to join

No sign-up. No registration. All sessions are streamed live and publicly on YouTube. Live captions for each session are available (see the video description on YouTube for the link).

Have a question for a presenter? No problem! Tweet your questions to @id24conf using the #id24q hashtag and our moderators will make sure they get them answered for you.
