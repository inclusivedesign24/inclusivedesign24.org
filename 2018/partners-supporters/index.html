Partners and supporters

Inclusive Design 24 (#id24) would not be possible without the kind help from our partners and supporters.

Barclays is a transatlantic consumer, corporate and investment bank anchored in our two home markets of the UK and the US. Our ambition is to become the most accessible and inclusive FTSE company and ensure that everyone can bank with or be employed by us regardless of their situation or circumstance. We're proud to work in partnership with groups focused on building and upskilling communities of product professionals such as a designers, developers, testers and researchers who can deliver brilliantly accessible and inclusive experiences. We fundamentally believe that accessibility and inclusive design education should be available to everyone and #id24 is a great platform for doing this.

TPG is an accessibility solutions provider passionately dedicated to helping government agencies, technology vendors, corporate firms, and educational institutions make their technology equally accessible to all people. They enable clients to achieve end-to-end accessibility in their digital assets and assist in embedding accessibility into processes and procedures. The company offers professional consulting, technology solutions, and services to ensure that clients reach all audiences effectively and efficiently while meeting commercial, governmental and international standards such as Section 508 and WCAG.
